Pizza

With real italian pizza, you will find homemade sauce made from freshly peeled tomatoes and a blend of savory, complimenting herbs.

Ingredients
  • Extra virgin olive oil.
  • Cornmeal (to help slide the pizza onto the pizza stone)
  • Tomato sauce (smooth or pureed)
Steps
  1. Gather Ingredients. Heat the sauce while chopping up these ingredients..
  2. Prepare the Dough. This step takes some practice.
  3. Add the Toppings... I like to layer the cheese then pepperoni last..
